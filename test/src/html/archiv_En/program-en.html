Program III International Agro Targi Wschód 8 – 9 June 2019 r.

Program III International Agro Targi Wschód 8 – 9 June 2019 r.

    1. Agricultural fair on 8-9 June 2019, at 9.00 - 18.00.
    2. June 8, at 11.00 - Official opening of the Fair.
    3. Advice for farmers will be conducted by:
        Governmental Agencies:
        - Agricultural Market Agency (KOWR),
        - The Agency for Restructuring and Modernisation of Agriculture (ARiMR),
        - Agricultural Social Insurance Fund (KRUS),
        National Research Institutes:
        - The Institute of Soil Science and Plant Cultivation,
        - National Research Institute of Animal Production in Balice,
        - Plant Breeding and Acclimatization Institute in Radzikow,
        - Institute of Agricultural and Food Biotechnology in Warsaw,
        - The Institute of Plant Protection - National Research Institute,
        - Research Institute of Horticulture in Skierniewice,
        - Institute of Agricultural and Food Economics National Research Institute,
        Agriculture Univercities,
        Agricultural Advisory Centers.
    4. Seminars for farmers will take place in the seminar tent.
    5. On the 8 and 9 June demonstration fields, professional demonstrations of the work of agricultural machines will take place,
    6. Exhibition plots of seed companies.
    7. Demonstrations of farm animals.
    8. Exhibition of antique agricultural machines,
    9. Competitions of agricultural knowledge - technical and professional skills of agricultural school students,
    10. A sector of flowers and decorative plants,
    11. 1st National Meeting of Users of Small Gardening Tractors
        - demonstrations of the work of tractors with associated machines,
        - competitions for the public,
    12. Many other attractions
    13. Exhibitors' Evening, 8 June, beginning of the chair 19.00, Kresowy Manor in Radymno.

Accompanying events of Agro Targi Wschód

    1. Demonstrations of modern technologies by the Rzeszów University of Technology:
        - Mars rover,
        - 3D printer,
        - glider,
        - Formula One Student car,
    2. University of Rzeszów, robot displays and archaeological site.
    3. Competition - promotion of rural self-governments - competition (attractive prizes),
    4. Contest competitions where trips are abroad,
    5. Handicraft festival,
    6. Shows of service dogs (police, Railway Protection Service, Border Guard).
    7. Artistic metalwork - minting of the trade fair coin.
    8. Busowycieczki - sightseeing of Przemyśl and the surrounding area.
    9. Lunapark - an amusement park, a wide offer on the area of approx. 0.6 ha.
    10. Falconry demonstrations (birds of prey).
    11. Military demonstrations:
        -5 Battalion of Podhale Rifles in Przemyśl - static demonstrations, military vehicles, equipment,  
        - 3 Podkarpackie Territorial Defense Brigade - demonstrations of the WOT soldiers' fitness,
The program of the event is subject to change, which will be immediately posted on the fair's website.
